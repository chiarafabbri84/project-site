Our work


The aim of our project was to enrich the entry of a culturally important monument or landmark on the ArCo knowledge graph. To begin, we first chose a topic. This process was started by looking for many monuments and important landmarks in the Emilia Romagna region, in order to find one that we both liked and could benefit from enrichment. To find an existing topic which presented gaps in its data, we looked on the ArCo Ontology and browsed the website with the ArCo SPARQL endpoint, and with the queries needed we explored Palazzo dei Diamanti in Ferrara. Our choice was also motivated by the fact that Ferrara is the birthplace of one of our teammates (Carolina).
There was also a comparative process in the meantime: as we carefully looked into the Palazzo dei Diamanti entry on ArCo, we compared it to the Teatro Regio of Parma entry. As the Teatro Regio entry had more information, we could easily understand which gaps were to be filled, and after a process of identification we went on with the LLM prompting. For this purpose, we used Chat GPT and Deepseek, which were particularly useful in researching the information that was needed, and on which we explored with the three prompting techniques studied in class.
Our second-to-last step was to construct the RDF triples to fill in the identified gaps.
