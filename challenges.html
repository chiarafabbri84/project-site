Our challenges
and how we overcame them


While working on our project, we were met with some challenges along the way. In this page, we will talk about the hardships we faced and how we sorted our problems out.


ü§ñLLM prompting


First of all, the LLMs were not always reliable. For example, the result we got with Chat GPT on the LLM prompting step of our project was incorrect, specifically with the Few-shot technique. Thankfully, the information retrieved in this process was easily fact-checked with the other LLM (DeepSeek), Wikipedia, or even re-trying with the same prompt to get a different answer.


Another issue we came in contact with when working with LLMs was with the triples, specifically in constructing the queries to add in ArCo SPARQL. Chat GPT added the keyword ‚ÄúBIND‚Äù in the queries multiple times, which did not work, and which DeepSeek avoided. Besides, both the LLMs did not know that ‚Äúex‚Äù is a placeholder, and when we were trying to construct the query for the committent of Palazzo dei Diamanti, they did not use the correct label. To overcome these problems, we had to manually check and correct the given queries, thanks to the notes from our course.


‚ùìSPARQL queries

Furthermore, we had to face the unfamiliarity of ArCo keywords. In the first part of our project, we had difficulties in understanding ArCo keywords and strict syntax while making the first couple of queries. To do get used to them, we had to check exsisting SPARQL patterns and queries to better our abilities in creating queries. Moreover, we used ChatGPT to help us adjusting some incorrect syntax for our queries.


üíªCSS file


Lastly, we encountered several challenges when constructing the CSS file of our website, specifically in the layout and formatting in terms of layout and style. Our problem came also because we wanted to create a formatting style that was different from the other templates we saw. We asked ChatGTP for help, and it gave us many solutions. Many of them were valuable, whereas others were not correct, so we had to go through many stages of trials and errors to fix it in the best of our capacities. Apart from Chat GPT, websites such as W3Schools were particularly helpful.
